# Zivana Labs

> Trust infrastructure for the African informal economy.

Hundreds of millions of people across Africa participate in informal 
economic activity — producing real value, serving real customers, 
building real reputations — yet remain invisible to formal capital 
markets. The barrier is not productivity. It is legibility.

Zivana is an open Layer 2 protocol built on Cardano and Midnight that 
makes economic capability visible, verifiable, and financeable without 
requiring informal actors to become formal first. Trust built in a 
Lagos market, a Nairobi cooperative, or an Accra trade network belongs 
to the people who earned it — and should travel with them.

---

## What we are building

**Zivana Protocol** is open infrastructure. Four primitives — Identity, 
Trust, Covenant, and Distribution — that any developer can build on to 
create trust-enabled financial applications for the informal economy.

**Catalyst** is the genesis application. A regenerative capital platform 
connecting Nigerian SMEs with investors through profit-sharing covenants 
that replace extractive lending with aligned, community-owned growth.

---

## Protocol Stack

| Layer | Technology | Role |
|---|---|---|
| Settlement | Cardano | eUTxO distribution, $ZVN token, finality |
| Privacy | Midnight Network | Shielded covenants, ZK proofs, selective disclosure |
| Identity | Hyperledger Identus | DID credentials, verifiable attestations |
